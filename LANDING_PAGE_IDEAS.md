# Landing Page Ideas: "Site Mais Barato do Brasil"

> Strategic document with persuasive triggers, conversion techniques, and structure for a high-converting landing page focused on selling the cheapest website service in Brazil.

---

## Executive Summary

- Average landing page converts at **2.35%**, top performers reach **11.45%+**
- **95%** of purchase decisions are subconscious
- Companies using psychological triggers can increase conversion by **14%** and revenue by **15%**
- Proper psychological framing can increase conversions by **up to 300%**

## 2. Psychological Triggers (Gatilhos Mentais)

### 2.1 Scarcity (Escassez)

- **Limited spots**: "Apenas 12 vagas este mês para garantir qualidade"
- **Stock indicator**: "3 vagas restantes para entrega esta semana"
- **Exclusive offer**: "Oferta exclusiva para quem chegou até aqui"

### 2.2 Urgency (Urgência)

- **Countdown timer**: "Preço especial válido por: 23h 47m 12s"
- **Deadline**: "Preço de lançamento só até dia 15"
- **Action prompt**: "Reserve sua vaga agora antes que o preço suba"

### 2.3 Social Proof (Prova Social)

- **Specific numbers**: "Mais de 847 negócios já confiam em nós" (avoid "muitos" or "centenas")
- **Testimonials**: 3–5 real testimonials with photo, name, occupation, city
- **Logos**: "Sites entregues para profissionais de: advocacia, saúde, beleza, consultoria..."
- **Reviews**: Average rating (4.8/5) with number of reviews

### 2.4 Authority (Autoridade)

- **Credentials**: Years in market, number of projects delivered
- **Recognition**: Mentions in media, partnerships
- **Expertise**: "Equipe com 10+ anos em desenvolvimento web"

### 2.5 Reciprocity (Reciprocidade)

- **Free value first**: "Baixe o guia grátis: 7 erros que matam sites de pequenos negócios"
- **Trial/Preview**: "Veja exemplos de sites já entregues" (gallery)
- **Consultation**: "Conversa gratuita de 15 min para entender seu negócio"

### 2.6 Loss Aversion (Aversão à Perda)

- **Frame around loss**: "Cada dia sem site = clientes que você perde para a concorrência"
- **Calculator**: "Quanto você deixa de faturar por mês sem um site?"
- **Before/after**: Show the cost of NOT having a site

### 2.7 Commitment (Compromisso)

- **Micro-commitments**: Start with "Quero saber mais" before "Quero fechar"
- **Progress**: Multi-step form with progress bar (Zeigarnik Effect)

## 3. Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring

- Show market price: ~~"Sites convencionais: R$ 2.500 a R$ 8.000"~~
- Our price: **R$ 97/mês** (or equivalent)
- Impact: Striking through old prices can increase conversion by **up to 40%**

### Decoy Effect

| Option | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Basic | R$ 49/mês | Decoy — limited features |
| **Popular** | R$ 97/mês | Target — best perceived value |
| Premium | R$ 197/mês | Anchor — makes middle option look cheap |

### Price Framing

- "Menos que 1 café por dia"
- "R$ 3,23 por dia"
- "Pagamento mensal — sem contratos longos"

## 4. Page Structure & Sections

### Above the Fold (First Screen)

- **Headline** (max 10 words)
- **Subheadline** (benefit + who it's for)
- **Primary CTA** (single, contrasting color)
- **Secondary CTA** (e.g., "Ver exemplos")
- **Hero image**: Real site examples, not abstract illustrations
- **Trust badge**: "Pagamento seguro" / "Sem cartão para começar"

### Section Order (AIDA + PAS)

1. **Attention**: Headline + hero
2. **Problem**: "Seu negócio está invisível na internet?"
3. **Agitation**: Expand pain (customers going to competitors, lost sales)
4. **Solution**: Our offer + how it works
5. **Social proof**: Testimonials + numbers
6. **Features/Benefits**: What's included (3–5 items max — cognitive chunking)
7. **Pricing**: Clear, with anchoring
8. **Objection handling**: FAQ (price, quality, support)
9. **Urgency**: Final CTA with scarcity/urgency
10. **Footer**: Contact, guarantees

### Cognitive Chunking

- Maximum **3–5 items** per section to avoid overload
- Use bullet points, not dense paragraphs
- F-pattern layout: Important content top-left, CTAs where eyes pause

## 5. Conversion Elements

### Primary CTA

- Action verbs: "Quero meu site agora", "Reservar minha vaga", "Começar em 5 minutos"
- Reduce friction: "Sem cartão de crédito", "Respondo em até 2h"
- Single CTA per section — Hick's Law: one purpose = less paralysis

### Form Optimization

- **Zeigarnik Effect**: Multi-step form with progress bar increases completion
- Minimum fields: Name, phone/email, type of business
- "Qual o principal desafio do seu negócio?" (qualifying, engagement)

### Von Restorff Effect

- CTA button must **visually contrast** with page (color, size)
- Green/orange for action vs. neutral background

---

## 6. Trust & Risk Reversal

- **Guarantee**: "7 dias de garantia — se não gostar, devolvemos 100%"
- **No commitment**: "Cancele quando quiser, sem multa"
- **Transparent pricing**: Show exactly what's included
- **Security badges**: PagSeguro, Mercado Pago, SSL
- **Contact visibility**: WhatsApp, email, phone

## 7. Brazilian Market Context

### Competitor Reference (2024)

- Freelancers: R$ 60–220/hora
- Agencies: R$ 2.500–8.000 one-time
- Monthly plans: R$ 49,90+ (Weyble), R$ 499 one-time (ToFlow)

### Our Positioning

- "Mais barato" with proof (comparison table)
- "Qualidade de agência, preço de iniciante"
- Focus on MEI, pequenos negócios, profissionais autônomos

## 8. Technical & UX

- **Page speed**: < 2.5s LCP — each second of delay = -7% conversion
- **Mobile-first**: 50%+ of traffic is mobile
- **Video**: 90s demo (muted autoplay) can increase conversion up to 80%
- **Sticky CTA**: Floating CTA on mobile after scroll

## 9. A/B Testing Priorities

1. **Headlines** (highest impact)
2. **CTA copy** ("Quero começar" vs. "Reservar vaga")
3. **Pricing display** (monthly vs. daily vs. comparison)
4. **Social proof placement** (early vs. before CTA)
5. **Form length** (short vs. multi-step)

---

## 10. Copy Guidelines

- **Outcome over feature**: "Clientes te encontram no Google" > "SEO incluído"
- **Customer language**: Use words from target audience
- **"So what?" test**: Every line should answer "e daí?" for the visitor
- **Authenticity**: Real testimonials, real numbers
